The Visioneer

Modupe Akinteye is the visionary founder of Hope For Me, a non-profit organization dedicated to restoring hope in underserved communities. Driven by her own experiences of hardship and resilience, Modupe started the NGO to give vulnerable children and families a chance at a better future.
